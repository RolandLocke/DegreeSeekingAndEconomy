DATA Information:

Applicants - Number of applications for admission from first-time, degree/certificate-seeking undergraduate students received by postsecondary institutions in the fall. Source:	nces.ed.gov 

Admissions - Percent of first-time, degree/certificate-seeking applicants to postsecondary institutions who were granted admission to enroll in the fall Source:		nces.ed.gov

https://nces.ed.gov/ipeds/TrendGenerator

Note: Data is collected from Title IV (those eligilbe to participate in the Federal Financial aid program) institutions in the US that do not have an open admissions policy. Applicants may apply to multiple programs and duplicates are not taken into account from these sources. 


GDP - Quarterly GDP data in billions of dollars Source: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/GDP

Inflation - Yearly consumer price inflation data Source: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/FPCPITOTLZGUSA
